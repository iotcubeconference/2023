About US

The 7th IoTcube Conference is a festival that introduces the technology of the 'IoTcube.net' platform, an automatic vulnerability analysis platform launched in 2016 through joint research among four countries: South Korea, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland. In 2023, the conference is organized with two main tracks: the technology trends of security in cross industries and the latest research achievements in Software Bill of Materials (SBOM). There will be a special unveiling of the SBOM generation experience feature, aiming to exchange ideas and find solutions with all those interested in supply chain security.
